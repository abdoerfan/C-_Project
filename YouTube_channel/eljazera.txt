It is a government television news channel, affiliated with the Al Jazeera Media Network. It was established on November 1, 1996, and its headquarters are located in the Qatari capital, Doha. Initially starting as a satellite channel for Arabic news and current affairs and since then with the same name “Al Jazeera”, the channel has expanded to become an international media network with a number of outlets, including the Internet and specialized television channels in multiple languages ​​in several regions of the world.
